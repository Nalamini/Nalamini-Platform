Privacy Policy for Nalamini Service Platform

Effective Date: December 2024

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates for taxi pickups and delivery services
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Razorpay (we don't store card details)
  • Documents: Photos for identity verification and service provider registration

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Service interactions, booking history, preferences
  • Camera Access: For document uploads and profile pictures only

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Processing taxi bookings, deliveries, and other services
  • Location Services: GPS data for accurate pickup/delivery locations
  • Communication: Service updates, booking confirmations, notifications
  • Payment Processing: Secure transaction handling through Razorpay
  • Account Management: User registration and profile management
  • Customer Support: Resolving issues and improving services

3. Information Sharing

We share information only as necessary:

  • Service Providers: Driver details for taxi bookings, delivery agent information
  • Payment Processor: Razorpay for secure payment processing
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law enforcement or legal proceedings
  • Business Operations: Aggregated, anonymized data for service improvement

We never sell personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • Database Security: Secure PostgreSQL database with access controls
  • Payment Security: PCI-compliant payment processing through Razorpay
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on need-to-know basis

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for legal compliance
  • Location Data: Deleted after 30 days unless required for dispute resolution

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a structured format
  • Opt-out: Disable location services or notifications (may limit functionality)

7. Children's Privacy

Nalamini is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover such information, it will be deleted immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Razorpay: Payment processing (governed by Razorpay's privacy policy)
  • YouTube: Management videos (governed by Google's privacy policy)
  • Google Maps: Location services (governed by Google's privacy policy)

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or email. Continued use constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

10. International Data Transfers

Data is primarily stored in India. Any international transfers comply with applicable data protection regulations and use appropriate safeguards.

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@nalamini.app
  • Address: Nalamini Services, Tamil Nadu, India
  • Response Time: We respond to privacy requests within 30 days

11. Governing Law

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of India and the Information Technology Act, 2000, and its rules including the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.
